About me

I began to work as a Business Analyst in 2018 for Carlson Wagonlit Travel, where I've quickly discovered my passion for programming, process automation, and database development.

I started post-graduate database studies in 2019 at the Warsaw University of Life Sciences to learn more about SQL and relational DBs. At the time, I was also learning the Python language for process automation and web-scraping, and Visual Basic for Application (VBA), to provide quick solutions and tools for my office colleagues.

In 2021 I joined Accrowell, an account solution providing company, to open a project for Polish Air Navigation Services Agency (PANSA). The project aimed to support the accounting, planning, and controlling teams to implement the ERP system in the future. My role as a Business Analyst was to back up the team with all the technical issues.

In 2022 I have left the BA role behind and focused on Software Development only. I joined PythON Academy Bootcamp by infoShare Academy, as well as 100 Days of Code - Python Pro Bootcamp by Dr. Angela Yu.

Today I'm a Python Developer in Warsaw, passionate about process automation, interested in Machine Learning and Backend Web-Development. Linux and Open Source enthusiast.
